Enterprise-Grade Security

Your clients' data security is our top priority. Therassist is built from the ground up with healthcare-grade security and compliance.

HIPAA Compliant

Full HIPAA compliance with Business Associate Agreements available for all customers.

End-to-End Encryption

All data encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). Zero plaintext storage.

Not a System of Record

Session recordings are deleted immediately after processing or at your discretion. We do not retain protected health information. Anonymized data contributions are fully de-identified.

Your Data, Your Control

Identifiable patient data is never used to train AI models without your consent. If you choose to contribute anonymized data to improve clinical tools, it is fully de-identified and cannot be linked to patients or sessions. Each practice's data is completely segregated.
