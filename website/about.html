Therassist brings together scientist-practitioner psychologists and AI technology pioneers to revolutionize mental healthcare delivery.

Our Mission

Our mission is twofold: to eliminate the administrative burden that keeps therapists from their core work, and to enhance clinical practice through intelligent documentation of evidence-based interventions.

By transforming routine documentation into a tool for clinical excellence, we help therapists deliver measurably better outcomes for their clients.

We believe that AI should amplify human expertise, not replace it. Every feature we build is designed to give clinicians more time for what matters most: the therapeutic relationship.

Our Story

Therassist was born from firsthand experience with the challenges of clinical documentation.

Our founder, Dr. David Lischner, is a psychiatrist, practice owner, and behavioral health tech entrepreneur. He co-founded Valant, the leading EHR for behavioral health, which he guided to market-leading growth.

Despite building one of the best EHRs in the industry, David saw that clinicians were still spending hours on documentation, falling behind on notes, and experiencing burnout. Even his own practice, EBTCS (Evidence-Based Treatment Centers of Seattle), which has a high concentration of evidence-based therapy experts, struggled with these challenges.

Therassist is David's answer to these problems. It combines his deep domain expertise in behavioral health technology with cutting-edge AI to finally deliver on the promise of truly intelligent clinical documentation.

The focus on evidence-based care isn't accidental. Unlike generic AI scribes, Therassist understands therapeutic modalities like CBT, DBT, and ACT because it was built by people who practice them every day.
